AMERICA'S AI ACTION PLAN

A Strategic Blueprint for Global AI Dominance and National Security

The Global Race for AI Supremacy

The "America's AI Action Plan" frames the pursuit of AI leadership as a critical national security imperative. The nation that pioneers the AI frontier will set global standards, reaping profound economic and military advantages. This plan is a whole-of-government strategy for the United States to win this race.

The Three-Pillar Strategy

I

Accelerate AI Innovation

Foster a thriving ecosystem for private-sector-led AI development by removing regulatory barriers and promoting American values in AI systems.

II

Build American AI Infrastructure

Address the critical need for robust data centers, semiconductor manufacturing, and the massive energy generation required to power them.

III

Lead in International Diplomacy

Export the American AI stack to allies while using stringent export controls and alliances to prevent misuse by adversaries.

Pillar I: Accelerating AI Innovation

Pillar I focuses on creating the optimal conditions for the U.S. private sector to innovate faster and more comprehensively than any global competitor. This involves a multi-pronged approach targeting regulation, workforce development, and strategic R&D investment.

Key Policy Action Areas

Regulatory Environment

Deregulation & Red Tape Removal:

Streamlining processes to accelerate AI development.

Values & Openness

Ensure AI Protects Free Speech and American Values:

Developing AI systems that uphold fundamental American principles.

Promote Open-Source & Open-Weight AI Models:

Encouraging transparent and accessible AI development.

Adoption & Application

Drive AI Adoption across Critical Sectors & Government:

Integrating AI tools into essential industries and federal operations.

Accelerate AI Adoption within Government:

Enhancing efficiency and effectiveness in public service through AI.

DOD AI Adoption:

Ensuring military preeminence through advanced AI integration.

Support Next-Gen Manufacturing with AI:

Leveraging AI to create advanced manufacturing capabilities.

Research & Development

Invest in AI-Enabled Science & Build World-Class Scientific Datasets:

Funding research and creating robust data for scientific breakthroughs.

Advance AI Interpretability, Control, & Robustness:

Improving understanding and reliability of complex AI systems.

Build a Comprehensive AI Evaluations Ecosystem:

Establishing robust methods for assessing AI system performance.

Protection & Trust

Protect Commercial & Government AI Innovations:

Safeguarding cutting-edge AI technologies from threats.

Combat Synthetic Media in the Legal System:

Addressing challenges posed by deepfakes and manipulated content.

Workforce Development

Empower the American Workforce for the AI Era:

Providing skills and training for jobs transformed by AI.

Pillar III: International Diplomacy & Security

American AI leadership must extend beyond its borders. Pillar III outlines a dual strategy: forging a global alliance by exporting American AI technology to partners, while simultaneously implementing robust controls to prevent adversaries from exploiting U.S. innovations.
